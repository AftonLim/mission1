What Is PopMart?

POP MART is a contemporary Chinese company specializing in the design, production, and distribution of collectible toys and lifestyle products. The company is known for its innovative and artistic approach to toy design, collaborating with various artists and designers to create unique and often whimsical collectibles.

Founded in 2010, POP MART has grown rapidly within the global designer toy community. They have expanded their offerings beyond toys to include a wide range of lifestyle products such as stationery, accessories, and homeware, all featuring their distinctive and colorful designs.

POP MART's products are often released in series or collections, and they are well-known for their blind box packaging. Blind boxes contain a surprise toy or figurine, encouraging collectors to collect and trade to complete their sets. This element of surprise and the artistic quality of their products have contributed to POP MART's popularity among toy collectors and enthusiasts worldwide.
